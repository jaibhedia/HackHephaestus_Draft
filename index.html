About

Hack Hephaestus is a 24-hour game development hackathon organised by the Vinyasa club of Dayananda Sagar Academy of Technology & Management. The hackathon will see students from some of the best Institutions across India, come together to build an amazing game. We are one of the student-run hackathon in the Bengaluru with a focus on Game Development!

Hack Hephaestus will host a week-day of fun, learning and innovation whilst letting students enjoy the capital of Karnataka. If you fancy an opportunity with free food, freebies, exciting getawways and the chance to gain prizes and meet our incredibly sponsors, don't hesitate to apply.

As part of Dayananda Sagar, we will be following DSATM's Code of Conduct.
